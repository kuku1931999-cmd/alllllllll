## Notes
The application requires a global Language Context to switch between English ('en') and Arabic ('ar').
RTL support is needed for Arabic layout.
Fonts: Playfair Display (Serif) for English Headings, Cairo for Arabic.
Colors: Soft pinks, purples, and white.
